CargoVR

Future websites will be more experiencial. This is an specialasion demo of the future of the design portfolio where you can experience their projects as they where.

Generally, designer faces issues while showing their portfolios or someone wanted to see designer's work. Videos have their own limitations. They are flat and they don't give the detailed experience of the form and space where this thing is actually presented.

New Media Design students have their profiles on cargocollective website. It is used to show the work done by students and it is really helpful for juniors to get the inspiration.

We have a module on AR/VR. In which, we have to find an application of AR and VR. After that, Implement them on different devices like HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, Samsung 360 or Asus Zenfone AR. Time duration was 48 hours.

We were a team of 4 people. We developed on HTC Vive with help of Unity. We wanted to create all profile of NMD students in 360 environments with their projects presented in the particular environment. Because environment plays a big role in experiential projects.
