Get, Set, Boat!

Due to the Global Warming, ice is melting. Sea level is also rising. It is a prototype of the futuristic boat which is collapsible for the youth of Sunk Mumbai in 2089. After retraction, this boat can be worn as a backpack.

The water level has started to increase. In future, there will be a possibility that the important cities of the world which are at the sea cost might start to sink. There will be water all around the city (max. 9-10 inches deep). What will be the futuristic transportation medium for that??

This was an imaginary condition we had given to solve in open elective subject 'Aqua City', conducted by Tamar Akov. Tamar Akov is an industrial designer specializing in sustainable design. Under her guidance, I made this design.

My age group was 18-25 year youth who want easily collapsible and quick open transportation medium. My target city was Mumbai. So, I optimized size after retraction as much as possible. I took help of origami to get out and get through this project in really short time with the full-scale model.
