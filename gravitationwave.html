Gravitation Wave

It is a Wind Driven Kinetic Scupture. Designed to give tribute to the amazing theory on its 100th aniversary.

Gravitational waves are ripples in the curvature of spacetime that are generated in certain gravitational interactions and propagate as waves outward from their source at the speed of light. First proposed by Henri Poincar√© in 1905, they were subsequently predicted in 1916 by Albert Einstein on the basis of his general theory of relativity.

There are 4 Universal forces in the universe where Gravity and Magnetism are included. They Work almost similarly in terms of attraction phenomena. Magnetism became a metaphoric representation of this work and I started exploring how magnets reacts.

After doing a lot research and number of iterations. I reached to this form which is in the video. There is no joints in whole sculpture and it stands by it's own on single point by deducting gravitational effect and gives very surreal feel. Hope you have enjoyed the video.
