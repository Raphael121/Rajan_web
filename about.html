About me

  • Education

    Currently, pursuing Masters in Design in New Media Design from NID. I have done B.Tech in Mechatronics, graduated in 2014 U.V.Patel College of Engineering. My schooling is done in my native city Junagadh. I was inclined towards engineering from childhood due to my family background. I was spending time in workshop and with machine during school time which gave me knowledge of practicle learning. I belive it is the core of my learning. I became keen reader.

  • Experiences

    I started doing projects from the first year of my engineering. We participated in international events like ABU Robocon for 2 years. I served team as Mechanical team head. We stand 12th and 17th among more than 100 national teams (including IITs and NITs) 2014 and 2013 respectively. We also won 'Best Rokkie Award'.

  • Indutrial Experiences

    I worked for two years as an Industrial Designer as well as Integration Engineer at Zenn Systems. They are basically for nuclear industry. Meanwhile, I have designed two commercial products which are on the process to come to market. Where I was looking the products from the conceptualizing to manufacturing. I have made hand sketches to mould 3D models. It gave me whole experience of product making and development processes.

    Worked as an Tech Learner-Academies for Avbodh Knowledge Foundation Ltd. At 9 BRD, Pune we had proposed a design for POD Loading Trolley for SU-30 MKI. which will be remote controlled and sensor positioned. At BEL, designed for a Small Form Factor (SFF) Server to be used by the Indian Army. Worked as an assistant to the Chief Technology Officer of KSD Technology for the same.

    As a responsible son, I am helping my father to design products for our family business. We are maker of hand carved wooden temple. you can checkout the work.

  • Social Work

    Under the influence of AIREP (All India Rural Empowerment Program), we have started a collegiate education movement known as 'GVM' (Ganpat Volunteaching Movement) where we go to teach nearby village students every sunday. This movement was started by around 10 students (including me) in 2013. Now, in 4 years, this movement has spread across 9 villages and more than 100 'VolunTeacher' go to tech every sunday. I am still part of this movement.
